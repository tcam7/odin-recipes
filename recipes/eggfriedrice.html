Egg Fried Rice

For this simple recipe you will need a coupe ingredients

  • Refrigerated rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  1. Cook Eggs
  2. Put rice in pan while on stove
  3. Add eggs and stir
  4. Add Sesame Oil, Oyster sauce and Soy sauce
  5. Stir till golden brown and Enjoy
